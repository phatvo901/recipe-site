Origin

The concept of frying chicken in Korea has itss beginning during the Korean War, when American troops stationed in South Korea during the late- 1940s, and the early- 1950s.

Varieties

Huraideu-chikin

often simply referred to as huraideu (후라이드), this is the basic fried chicken

Ganjang-chikin

fried chicken topped with or smothered with a large amount of thinly shredded scallions.

Padak

fried chicken coated in ganjang-based sweet and savoury sauce, which is often also garlicky.
